Articles Notes Teaching Other







My research is on elliptic partial differential equations of fully nonlinear type that appear in geometric problems, for instance, prescribed curvature. A classical problem of this type is the Minkowski problem. I am also interested in the geometry of hypersurfaces in both Riemannian and semi-Riemannian manifolds.
